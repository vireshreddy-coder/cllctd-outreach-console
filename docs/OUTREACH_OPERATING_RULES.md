# Outreach operating rules

Use this for daily outreach.

Core rule:
Lead with what cllctd has.
Do not lead with the buyer's gap.

Default asset line:
cllctd has rights-cleared first-person task video from real worksites, captured by a contributor network and packaged for physical AI training. Audio is supporting context, not the headline.

Best first buyer segments:
1. Robotics foundation model labs.
2. Humanoid robot companies.
3. Warehouse and factory robotics companies.
4. Home robot companies.
5. Surgical and precision manipulation robotics.
6. Autonomous systems teams that need messy real-world video.

Daily flow:
1. Pick 10 to 15 high-fit targets.
2. Verify website and contact route.
3. Generate one draft at a time.
4. Edit manually.
5. Run lint.
6. Send manually from the chosen founder account.
7. Mark sent.
8. Add a follow-up date.

Do not do this:
- Do not blast the whole list.
- Do not send from a fresh domain at high volume.
- Do not use old v1 copy.
- Do not lead with India.
- Do not promise payment timing.
- Do not include financing, company-worth, pricing, or split language.
- Do not use guessed personal emails.

Good CTA options:
- Worth a look?
- Should I send the sample reel?
- Want a small task-matched sample?
- Open to a 15-minute look?

Good subject options:
- first-person task video for physical AI
- rights-cleared task footage
- real worksite video for robot training
- cllctd sample reel

Sample body:

Hey [Name],

cllctd has rights-cleared first-person task video from real worksites.

It covers industrial and residential manipulation, captured by contributors and reviewed before delivery.

The data is built for physical AI teams that need messy real-world tasks, not clean lab footage.

We can cut a small sample around the task families you care about.

Worth a look?

V
